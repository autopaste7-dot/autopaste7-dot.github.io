## Basic troubleshooting

### GPS accuracy
For best results:

- make sure Location Services are enabled
- use the app in an open area when possible
- allow time for GPS signal to stabilize
- remember that phone-based GPS readings can vary by conditions and signal quality

### Purchase restore
If premium content does not appear after reinstalling or changing devices, try restoring purchases from inside the app.

### App updates
Keep the app updated to access the latest improvements, fixes, and new features.
