# GPS Speedometer・Speed Tracker - Privacy Overview

This page provides a simple privacy overview for **GPS Speedometer・Speed Tracker**.

## Privacy summary

GPS Speedometer・Speed Tracker is designed to work without creating an account.

The app may use location access to provide real-time GPS speed tracking, route mapping, and trip statistics.

## Key privacy points

- no account is required
- no registration is required
- location is used to calculate speed and trip routes
- trip-related data may be stored locally on the device
- premium purchases may be handled through Apple's in-app purchase system
- the app may use limited third-party services related to subscriptions, crash reporting, analytics, or advertising

## Examples of app functionality that may rely on permissions

### Location
Used for:

- real-time speed measurement
- route tracking
- trip statistics
