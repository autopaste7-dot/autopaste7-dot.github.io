# GPS Speedometer・Speed Tracker

Official website for **GPS Speedometer・Speed Tracker**, an iPhone app that helps users track their speed in real time using GPS.

## What the app does

GPS Speedometer・Speed Tracker turns your iPhone into a clean and easy-to-read speedometer for driving, cycling, motorcycle trips, travel, and general movement tracking.

The app is designed for users who want:

- real-time GPS speed tracking
- a large and readable speed display
- digital or analog speedometer modes
- HUD mode for windshield viewing at night
- trip route tracking on a map
- distance, max speed, and average speed statistics
- unlimited trip history
- support for both MPH and KMH

## Core features

### Real-time GPS speedometer
Track your current speed live using your iPhone's GPS.

### Digital and analog views
Switch between a bold digital readout and a classic analog dial.

### HUD mode
Use mirrored display mode for easier windshield viewing during night driving.

### Trip route map
See the route of each trip directly on the map.

### Detailed statistics
Review trip distance, top speed, and average speed after each session.

### Unlimited history
Save and revisit previous trips and speed data.

### MPH / KMH support
Change units anytime depending on your region or preference.

### Simple Mode
Use a minimal screen layout with very large numbers for easier viewing while moving.
